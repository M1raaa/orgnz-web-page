First version:

ORGNZ is an innovative and user-friendly mobile application, currently in development, that is set to revolutionize the way you manage your everyday products.

All the way from make-up products, medicine, food items and even your devices.

  • It allows you to create an inventory of all your products.
  • You will see very easily what are the products that will expire soon.
  • When in need to buy more products, you can filter and search for specifics items.
  • Stay informed about your inventory with detailed statistics and insights.
  • If you keep an inventory of products from multiple houses, you can set different locations for each and every item.

Next updates:

We're not basic. We're aiming for the moon with this app, so be ready for a clutter-free, waste-free lifestyle with us:

  • You will simply scan the product barcode so you won't have to manually enter all the details.
  • You could attach a photo for easier identification of the products.
  • You will receive notifications as items approach their expiration dates, ensuring you use them before they go to waste.
  • When you will add a new medicine item you could set the amount needed and the frequency of treatment. Depending on the amount of medicine left, you will be notified to buy a new box on your next trip to the store.
  • Access your inventory and data across multiple devices.
